À propos

I'm a bilingual Machine Learning Engineer with over 9 years of experience delivering AI-driven solutions across a wide range of industries, including media, finance, energy, transportation, retail, environmental services, and public health. My expertise lies in designing scalable machine learning pipelines, deploying real-world AI applications, and conducting impactful research in computer vision and natural language processing (NLP). My academic and research journey spans prestigious institutions in Canada and internationally. I completed my M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence at ÉTS Montréal, where I developed a novel framework for Dynamic Multi-view Learning using Large Language Models (LLMs), applied to fake news detection and text classification. During my graduate studies, I was awarded two IVADO scholarships for excellence in AI research. Previously, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Applied Statistics from Shahid Beheshti University, where I also worked as a Lecturer and Research Assistant. In addition to my applied industry experience, I remain deeply engaged with the academic and research community, contributing to cutting-edge AI research across multiple domains.
